Why “Essay It Out Loud”?

The name comes from one of the simplest and most effective writing techniques I know: read your words out loud.

When you hear your writing, you hear the rhythm. You hear where it flows. You hear where it doesn't.

Most importantly, you hear your voice.

And that's what this process is really about.

Finding it.
